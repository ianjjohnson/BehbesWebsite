Turn that frown upside down

Medical decision making is difficult; making decisions for infants who cannot communicate their needs is very difficult. Behbes changes that. Here at Behbes, we believe that transparent, thorough, and accessible information regarding the health of our infants is an invaluable resource which should be shared with all. Our mission is to simplify the urgent information regarding infant healthcare into a universally accessible and relatable resource for new parents.

Our Moms

Behbes is proud to have the best “Mom Team” around. Our organization of moms and scientists, all hardened experts in their fields of motherhood and infant medicine, work tirelessly to deliver the best, most accessible and useful medical information for Behbes. We are confident that these battle-tested mothers-in-arms will bring you the relevant and informative assistance that you need when you and your Behbe need it most.

Healthy Behbes

Studies have shown that the children parents who actively involve themselves in the healthcare of their children, from birth to adolescence, have more positive physical and mental health outlooks than their peers. Behbes can help carry the burden of infant healthcare to help insure the best future for your little angels.

Behbes App

Behbes lets users describe their Behbe’s symptoms to find likely causes and treatments, and it also provides a simple search feature where new parents can search for specific key-words or phrases that describe their Behbe’s condition. While Behbes is not a catch-all replacement for pediatric medical care, it can help alleviate the burden of the day-to-day ailments that occur in new Behbes.
